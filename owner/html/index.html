Welcome to FoodShare!

FoodShare is a platform dedicated to reducing food waste by connecting restaurants and individuals with local NGOs that can distribute food to those in need.

Whether you're a restaurant owner wanting to donate leftover food or an NGO looking for donations, our easy-to-use platform helps you do it quickly and efficiently.
